City XR Data Project

Data Framework and Presentation

What is City XR?

The City XR Data Project aims to provide a visual platform for maps geographical data. Our goal is to provide framework through which users can view historical and real-time geodata through which patterns and trends can be identified. Our goal is to provide accurate data from reliable sources, while also allowing users and groups to upload and host their own data to work with the City XR framework.

Features

Data Types

Easily view various types of historical and real-time geodata, such as:

  • Climate Data
  • Crime Rates
  • Public Transit Routes
  • Businesses
Data Sources

The City XR project will host frequently updated databases containing various data types from multiple reliable sources, but will also offer the functionality for users to upload their own datasets for personal or professional usage.
